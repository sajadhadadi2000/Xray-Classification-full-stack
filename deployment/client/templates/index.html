Prediction of chest radiology

We use a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) model to predict chest radiology diseases by analyzing X-rays or CT scans. The model identifies patterns indicative of conditions like pneumonia or lung cancer, enhancing diagnostic speed and accuracy, and assisting radiologists in providing timely and effective patient care.
